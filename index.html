Research and Publications

The Making of Moodify: Weaving AI Technologies into a Personalized Mental Health Tapestry
IJRAR International Journal of Research and Analytical Reviews · Apr 29, 2024
Moodify is an Android application focused on mental health support for young adults aged 20 to 35. It combines three core systems: 1 an emotion detection module using Google ML Kit facial analysis to predict mood and recommend tailored music and podcasts 2 an autonomous chatbot powered by OpenAI API with speech recognition and text to speech for confidential support 3 anonymous group chat rooms backed by Redis Cloud DB to let users talk safely without revealing identity.
